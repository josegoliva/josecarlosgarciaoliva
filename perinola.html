Games People Play

Specification of the piece:

Projection of a video in a loop
and two pieces sculpted in wood
20cm x 6cm (perinola head)
19cm x 4cm (perinola stick)


Description:

The uncertainty of the perinola can keep people waiting. It gives you hope to achieve it, and at the same time play with your frustration. It is one of the traditional games of Latin America and a portrait of its timeless social-political scenario.The constant duality between failure and victory.
