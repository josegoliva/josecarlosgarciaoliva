Mausoleum

Specification of the piece:

Printed on recycled paper. 6 spreads.


Description:

Mausoleum is a typographic system that replaces the name of Nicolas Maduro by a list of the registered people killed during protests by National Bolivarian Armed Forces of Venezuela.


The Observer is an independent publication using the Mausoleum typographic system. Is composed of a collection of different sources of newspapers around the world about the current social-political situation of Venezuelan folks.
